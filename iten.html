7-Day Itinerary: Chennai → Madurai → Rameswaram → Kanyakumari

Day 1: Arrival in Chennai

  • Arrival: Reach Chennai and check-in.
  • Sightseeing: Visit Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple.

Day 2: Chennai → Madurai

  • Departure: Drive to Madurai.
  • Sightseeing: Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal.

Day 3: Madurai → Rameswaram

  • Departure: Drive to Rameswaram.
  • Sightseeing: Ramanathaswamy Temple, Pamban Bridge, Dhanushkodi Beach.

Day 4: Rameswaram → Kanyakumari

  • Departure: Drive to Kanyakumari.
  • Sightseeing: Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar Statue.

Day 5: Kanyakumari Full Day

  • Morning: Watch sunrise at Vivekananda Rock Memorial.
  • Sightseeing: Kanyakumari Temple, Sunset Point.

Day 6: Kanyakumari → Madurai

  • Return: Drive back to Madurai.
  • Sightseeing: Optional visit to Sri Meenakshi Temple.

Day 7: Madurai → Chennai

  • Return: Drive back to Chennai for departure.
